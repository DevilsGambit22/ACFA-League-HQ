Inside League HQ

Official Communication: Follow commissioner announcements, schedule notices, event instructions, and rulings.
Club Coordination: Representatives organize their teams and communicate league information to their members.
Competition: Approved clubs compete in recurring ACFA events hosted through Chess.com.
League Record: Results, champions, awards, standings, and historic accomplishments are maintained by League HQ.

League Rules & Operations

League HQ members represent their clubs and are expected to protect the fairness, organization, and sportsmanship of every ACFA competition.

Sportsmanship: Treat opponents, organizers, and representatives with respect.
Representation: Each club maintains an authorized representative who receives and relays official information.
Event Compliance: Follow published schedules, formats, eligibility requirements, and event instructions.
Fair Play: All activity remains subject to Chess.com Fair Play policies and platform rules.
Disputes: Submit concerns privately to League Administration with clear supporting information.
Official Decisions: Commissioner and League HQ rulings govern recorded league outcomes.

About the ACFA League

The ACFA League brings Chess.com clubs together through organized, welcoming, and competitive events. It gives club communities a consistent place to represent their teams, meet other clubs, and pursue shared seasonal achievements.

League HQ serves as the operational center for approved club representatives, official communication, competition rules, event coordination, standings, awards, and championship history.
